    Rhythm & Timing

    As you play a song, you'll earn a GOOD, OK, or BAD for each note, depending on how well the note was lined up with the frame when you hit it.
    If your timing is perfect, you'll get a GOOD.
    If you're even a little off rhythm, you'll get an OK. If you missed it completely, you'll get a BAD.
    Stay on rhythm to get all GOODs and rack up a high score!
    You can get a "Score Rank" by achieving the certain score.
    There are 7 Score Ranks in total, including 【粋】 Iki, 【雅】 Miyabi, and 【極】 Kiwami.
    Ranks listed below are in ascending order from left to right.

    Also, you can get a Clear Crown by Clear Crown by clearing a song, and a Full Combo Crown by performing a Full Combo.
    You can also get a Donderful Combo Crown by getting a GOOD rating on every music note.
